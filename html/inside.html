HOW TO BAKE SALMON IN THE OVEN

  1. Arrange salmon fillets and broccoli florets on a lightly greased sheet pan.
  2. Prepare the butter sauce.
  3. Rub the minced garlic over the salmon fillets and pour the butter mixture over the salmon and broccoli.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice over everything and season with salt and pepper.
  5. In terms of how long to bake salmon: You should bake salmon in a preheated oven for 15 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
